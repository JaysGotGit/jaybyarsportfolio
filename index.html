About Me

Hi, I’m Jay Byars — an AWS-certified Cloud Coach with a deep-rooted passion for explaining the concepts you need in order to have a successful career in the cloud. I hold a degree in Computer Science and bring over 25 years of experience as a software trainer, helping individuals and teams understand and apply complex technology with confidence. Over the years, my focus has evolved from software training to cloud computing — and today, I specialize in Amazon Web Services (AWS). With multiple AWS certifications under my belt, I not only work with the cloud — I teach it, simplify it, and help others succeed with it. Whether you're just starting your cloud journey or preparing for your next AWS certification, my goal is to make learning the cloud clear, accessible, and impactful. I believe in sharing knowledge and building confidence (as well as applications) to people who are excited about the future of cloud. Thanks for stopping by — I’m glad you’re here.
